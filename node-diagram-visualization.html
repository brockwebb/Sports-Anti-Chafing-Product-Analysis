Anti-Chafe Product and Indregient Explorer

Instructions: "double-click" to highlight neighbor nodes, "double-click" again to restore. Click and drag nodes to other locations around the screen.

Motivations for this tool

Look at the relationships between anti-chafing products and ingredients to find and uncover useful patterns. Find the best anti-chafe products at the lowest price for use in ultramarathons.
